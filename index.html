ALIEN COLLECTON

Recreate iconic alien scenes with the Alien Collection. Generate swarms of Xenomorphs and Facehuggers, spread Alien Eggs, and drag-and-drop fully animated models for instant scene building. No animation experience required—just unleash your creativity and effortlessly bring iconic alien moments to life with ease.

FEATURES

Alien Swarms

Easily generate swarms of Xenomorphs and Facehuggers to recreate iconic scenes from the Alien films.

All-Surface Movement

Alien creatures can crawl on any surface—walls, ceilings, or floors—just like in the Alien films.

Pre-Animated Models

All models come pre-animated! Simply drop them into your scene and watch the action unfold, no need to animate them yourself.

Jumping

With just a simple toggle, your Facehuggers can leap and lunge at their targets, mimicking their signature movements.

Powerful Controls

Influence your swarm of with features like attraction, avoidance, follow curve, and more, giving you powerful ways to control their behavior and movement.

Swarm Presets

Easily import ready-made creature behaviors for hassle-free setup.

Bonus Alien Eggs

Includes Alien Eggs so you can design your own alien nest scenes.

Geo-Swarm Compatible

This collection is compatible with the Geo-Swarm addon for convenient swarm management, though its use is optional and not required.

NOTE: Geometry nodes does not yet support collisions. Some adjustments may be needed to avoid clipping.
